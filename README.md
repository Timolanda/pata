# pata
PATA - Africa’s Ultimate AR Treasure Hunt 🎯🌍

We’re building PATA, Africa’s biggest Augmented Reality (AR) treasure hunt, where players explore real-world locations—museums, markets, restaurants, nature trails, and historic sites—to discover on-chain NFT treasures, earn PATA Points, and unlock exclusive real-world rewards.

PATA transforms exploration into an interactive game, blending African history, culture, and adventure with modern blockchain technology. Whether you’re hunting for ancient relics, mythical artifacts, or hidden market gems, every discovery connects you deeper to Africa’s rich heritage.

PATA creates a seamless, gamified experience for both players and business partners looking to drive engagement through AR-powered sponsorships and treasure placements.
